MARKET OUTLOOK

The chip relief rally is real and Micron's strategic customer agreement disclosures suggest the AI memory boom has more durability than skeptics credit — that's multiple expansion territory. That said, the bull case has a meaningful shadow: AI capex is increasingly funded by capital markets rather than operating cash flow, meaning any equity volatility directly threatens the infrastructure buildout. The 1990s analog breaks down fast when you factor in fiscal expansion, deglobalization, and sticky inflation — the tailwinds of that era are now headwinds. Oil's continued slide toward $72 is the most constructive macro development on the board; if it holds, yields follow and the Fed's hand gets easier.
