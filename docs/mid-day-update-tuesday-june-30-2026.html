COMPANY NEWS

AVAV (AeroVironment) is spiking after a strong FQ4: EPS of $1.84 vs. Street $1.41, EBITDA $140MM vs. $126MM, revenue $641.6MM vs. $556.4MM, and a book-to-bill of 1.4x. F27 EBITDA guidance of $315MM came in light vs. the $346MM consensus, but the market is forgiving given the beat quality.

CNXC (Concentrix) is down more than 20% after reporting FQ2 EPS/revenue in line but slashing full-year guidance — now sees EPS of $10.83–$11.18 (vs. prior $11.48–$12.07) and FX-neutral sales growth of +0.25–1.25% (vs. prior +1.5–3%). The kicker: CNXC trades at ~2x earnings with a free cash flow yield above 50%, and the market still doesn't care. Valuation is not a floor for legacy software/services.
