COMPANY NEWS

BBY (Best Buy) beat Q1 expectations with EPS $1.28 vs. Street $1.22, driven by comps of +2% vs. Street +0.9% and operating margin expansion of ~30 basis points to 4.1%. Management says May comps tracking 7-9% month-to-date.

LLY (Eli Lilly) surged after Zepbound returned to CVS Caremark formulary following agreed price cuts (Bloomberg).

MRVL (Marvell) posted inline Q1 results but boosted guidance again, noting cloud capex growth moderating in F28 and planning ~$1B in component prepayments this fiscal year.

NSC/UNP (Norfolk Southern/Union Pacific) both fell after federal Surface Transportation Board paused review of their $71.5B merger to "thoroughly evaluate" the application (WSJ).
